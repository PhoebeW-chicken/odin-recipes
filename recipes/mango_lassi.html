Spintzofai

A soothing mango lassi for a hot summer's day!

This Indian drink will cool you down and fill you up with healthy vitamins and minerals

Ingredients

  • 2 mangoes
  • 1 cup yoghurt (Greek)
  • 1.4 tsp cardamom
  • 3/4 cups milk

Steps to Make

  1. Chill the ingredients: Keep all your ingredients cool. Dice the mango up.
  2. Blend everything up:Add everything except the yoghurt to a cleaned blender, and blend until smooth. Then blend in the yoghurt until you achieve a smooth, even, velvety consistency.
  3. Pour and serve:Enjoy in a chilled glass.
