Spintzofai

A spicy mix of hot sausages and pepper!

This traditional greek dish will warm up and fill your kitchen with aromatic scents!

Ingredients

  • Greek sausage (loukanifo)
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Capsicums/Bell peppers
  • Tomatos (diced into chunks)
  • Tomato paste
  • Chilli flakes and paprika (for some spice!)
  • Herbs (oregano - get dried greek oregano - and fresh parsley)
  • Ouzo (for some fresh anise flavours!)

Steps to Make

  1. Cook the sausage:Slice the sausage into pieces. Heat up olive oil over medium high heat in a frying pan, add the sausages and cook for 15 minutes, turning over halfway through. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  2. Cook the sauce:Add onions to oil, and saute for about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and capsicum and cook for 5 minutes. Finally add the tomato and tomato paste and reduce the heat and cook, stirring occasionally, for a further 5 minutes.
  3. Season and serve:Add all the spices and herbs, return the sausage to the pan, and cook until the capsicum has softened. Finally add the ouzo, stir, and cook a little before serving immediately with steamed white rice!
